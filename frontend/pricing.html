02FAQ

Common questions.

How accurate is the age estimate?

The underlying ResNeXt-50 model achieves a mean absolute error below 2 years on a held-out Malaysian multi-ethnic test set (n = 12,400). Every report shows the point estimate alongside its bootstrap-derived 95% confidence interval.

Are the reports admissible in court?

OPG Vision reports are decision-support documents and should be reviewed by a qualified forensic odontologist before use in legal proceedings. Every report includes a full methodology section and disclaimer; borderline results always require specialist sign-off before release.

Is OPG image data stored after analysis?

No image data is retained after inference. Only report metadata (case ID, estimated age, classification, model version) is persisted to your dashboard. OPG datasets are classified as biometric sensitive information under PDPA Amendment Act 2024 — Professional and Enterprise clients on on-premises deployment retain full data residency control.

What file formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and DICOM (.dcm) files up to 10 MB. Full DICOM metadata parsing — including SOP Instance UID extraction — is on the Pro roadmap.

Is on-premises deployment available?

Yes — Professional and Enterprise clients can elect on-premises deployment to meet MOH data sovereignty requirements and PDPA Amendment Act 2024 compliance. OPG datasets remain entirely within your institution's network at all times.
