Privacy Policy.

Effective 1 January 2026 · PDPA Amendment Act 2024 compliant

1. Data we collect

OPG Vision collects your name, work email address, and the case metadata you submit during an analysis (case ID, reference number, officer name, estimated age result, classification). OPG radiograph images are processed in memory only and are never stored after inference completes.

2. How we use your data

Case metadata is stored to power your dashboard history, enable report re-download, and provide audit logs for your organisation. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for any commercial purpose.

3. Data residency

Cloud-hosted accounts have data stored on Malaysian servers. Professional and Enterprise clients on on-premises deployment retain full data residency control within their own infrastructure — no data leaves your network.

4. Biometric data

OPG radiographs are classified as biometric sensitive information under PDPA Amendment Act 2024. We apply the maximum retention controls required by law: images are deleted immediately after inference, and case results are stored only with your explicit consent.

5. Your rights

Under PDPA 2024 you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@opgvision.ai and we will respond within 14 working days.
